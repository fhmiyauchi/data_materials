## 2. Goals and Assumptions

### OBJECTIVE
Build a scalable Material Intelligence pipeline capable of extracting, structuring, enriching, and interpreting material-related data from heterogeneous sources to enable downstream scientific analysis, searchability, and AI-driven insights.

### WORKING ASSUMPTIONS
- This is an excersise and we will be using mock data to demonstrate the pipeline and data interpretations
- The data will come from different sources and formats and need to be "cleaned" and "normalized".
- The process can be scalable to handle large datasets after being adjusted for specific needs. 
- Arbitrary scope and analysis/reports were selected for the purpose of this excersise.
- Data governance, security, privacy, and compliance is not part of this excersise besides would be essential in a real world scenario
